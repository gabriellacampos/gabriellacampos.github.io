The Best Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 5 Avocado
  • 5 Roma Tomatoes
  • 1/4 Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Lemon Juice
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Peel, pit, and mash avocado in a medium bowl
  2. Add lemon juice to prevent browning
  3. Chop tomato, onion, pepper, and cilantro
  4. Add chopped ingredients to bowl with the mashed avocado
  5. Add salt to mixture
  6. Mix ingredients evenly
  7. Ready to serve!
